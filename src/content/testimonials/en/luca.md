The first word that comes to mind after this 10-day experience aboard Ikigaisailing is THANK YOU. A fundamental disconnect from the “matrix.” Thank you for your professionalism, but above all for the empathy you managed to establish from the first time. Your ability to listen and share your stories on board, even over a good glass of wine. See you soon again.
