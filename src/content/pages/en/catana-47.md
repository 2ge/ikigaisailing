_The Catana 47_ is designed to enjoy comfort and space without giving up the thrill of sailing and the sea. It is a high-performance catamaran capable of maintaining high speeds on long crossings and moving easily even in light winds, while its spacious interiors guarantee comfort in all weather conditions. It can accommodate 6 to 7 people in 4 cabins.

[Take the virtual tour →](https://www.cruisingworld.com/sailboats/catana-47/)

## Cabins & layout

- **2 double cabins** — configurable with two single beds or one large double bed
- **1 cabin** with a double bed
- **A fourth cabin** converted into a large bathroom with a panoramic shower for two
- **2 electric toilets**, one on each hull

At the bow are the **crew cabin**, the **sail locker**, and a dedicated **storage cabin** for sports equipment. At the stern, the **large cockpit table** lowers to create another spacious double bed.

## Galley

Since we are passionate about good food — and former professional restaurateurs — the kitchen plays a central role on board. It is equipped with:

- **Bread maker** — because fresh bread every day is priceless
- **Magma XL barbecue** — the star of countless cookouts, especially after a lucky fishing trip
- **4-burner stove**, stainless-steel gas oven, fridge, freezer, dishwasher, fresh-pasta machine, blender and more
- **Automatic coffee machine** with integrated grinder
- **Drinking-water system** dispensing sparkling or still water, cold or at room temperature

## Energy & autonomy

In terms of energy, we are completely self-sufficient:

- **12 solar panels**
- **Hydroturbine**
- **600 Ah lithium batteries**
- **5 kW diesel generator**

We also carry **2 watermakers** producing **150 L/h**, plus a **washing machine**.

## Sports equipment

On board you will also find:

- **25 hp dinghy**
- **3-person kayak**
- **SUP** (stand-up paddleboard)
- Snorkelling, freediving and spearfishing gear
- Full **scuba diving** equipment
- **Kitesurfing** gear
- Yoga mats

## Sails

New in 2017:

- **Mainsail** — 86 m² Hydranet 483/433, 5 battens, 3 reefing points, with indicators; Harken T32 ball-bearing cars; lazy bag (new 2021)
- **Genoa** — 53 m² triradial cut in Hydranet 433, UV protection strip
- **Gennaker** — 130 m² on a furling boom, Stormlite blue fabric, anti-torsion cable, UV strip
- **Code 0** — 81 m² on a furling boom, laminated carbon-aramid, anti-torsion cable, CZ60/30 UV fabric
- A **second set in Dacron** — mainsail and genoa

## Electronics

Raymarine, new in 2017:

- **Axiom 9 chartplotter** (2020), Multi i70, VHF RA60, 2× wind display i60, 1× Multi i70, 11× p70 autopilot console
- **Primary autopilot** — Raymarine ACU 400 with L&S hydraulic cylinder
- **Emergency autopilot** — Raymarine with L&S hydraulic cylinder
- **Quantum radar** and **AIS 650 Class B** transceiver
