## CATANA 47

CATANA 47

_The Catana 47_ is designed to enjoy comfort and space without giving up the thrill of sailing and the sea. It is a high-performance catamaran capable of maintaining high speeds on long crossings and moving easily even in light winds, while its spacious interiors guarantee comfort in all weather conditions. It can accommodate 6 to 7 people in 4 cabins.

[Virtual tour](https://www.catana.com/visite/Catana-47/Catana-47.html)

[](https://www.ikigaisailing.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/catana-47-e1737790771595.jpg)

The boat features:
- **2 double cabins** (configurable with two single beds or one large double bed)
- **1 cabin with a double bed**
- **A fourth cabin converted into a large bathroom** with a panoramic shower for two people
- **2 electric toilets** , one on each hull

At the bow, there is the **crew cabin** , the**sail locker** , and an entire **storage cabin** for sports equipment. At the stern, the **large cockpit table** can be lowered to create another spacious double bed.

## KITCHEN:

Since we are passionate about good food and former professional restaurateurs, the kitchen plays a central role on board. It is equipped with:

Bread Maker – because baking fresh bread daily is priceless

### Magma XL Barbecue – the star of countless barbecues, especially after a lucky fishing trip

### 4-burner stove, stainless steel gas oven, fridge, freezer, dishwasher, fresh pasta machine, blender, etc.

### Automatic coffee machine with an integrated grinder

### Drinking water system capable of dispensing sparkling or still water, cold or at room temperature
