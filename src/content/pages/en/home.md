SAILING AROUND

THE WORLD

Our journey began in the Mediterranean in **the summer of 2022** , departing from Rome with stops in Sardinia, Spain, the Canary Islands, and Cape Verde before crossing the Atlantic and reaching the Caribbean in January 2023. Since then, we have explored the Lesser Antilles from the BVI to Grenada, then Los Roques in Venezuela and the Dutch Antilles.

Our future itinerary will take us to Colombia, **the Panama Canal, and the Pacific, passing through the Galápagos, French Polynesia, Fiji, Indonesia,** until we reach Asia and eventually return to the Mediterranean via the Red Sea.

**Join us to experience the thrill of sailing into the unknown and discovering the world from a unique perspective.**
