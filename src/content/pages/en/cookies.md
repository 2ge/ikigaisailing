## The use of cookies

### Type of cookies we use

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

We use both session and persistent cookies for the purposes set out below:

- **Necessary / essential cookies** (session, administered by us) — essential to provide you with services available through the Website and to enable you to use some of its features. Without these cookies, the services you have asked for cannot be provided.
- **Functionality cookies** (persistent, administered by us) — allow us to remember choices you make when you use the Website, such as your language preference, to give you a more personal experience.
- **Analytics & performance cookies** (persistent, administered by third parties) — used to understand traffic to the Website and how visitors use it, so we can improve it. We use Google Tag Manager and a privacy-friendly analytics tool for this.

### Your choices regarding cookies

If you prefer to avoid the use of cookies on the Website, you can disable cookies in your browser and delete the cookies saved by your browser for this website. If you do not accept our cookies, you may experience some inconvenience in your use of the Website and some features may not function properly. Your browser's help pages explain how to delete or refuse cookies (Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge all document this).
