### 1. Booking Policy:

The activities and experiences offered by our organization are exclusively reserved for members of the sports association. We are an association that promotes activities related to sailing, kitesurfing, freediving, and other marine sports disciplines. Our goal is to offer unique experiences to those who share our passion for the sea and sports.

At the time of booking one of our experiences, participants who are not yet members of the association must apply for membership. The membership fee, which includes the first-year membership card, is already included in the contribution requested for the specific experience. This ensures that all participants have full access to the activities and benefits reserved for members.

Becoming a member of the association is not just a formal requirement but also a commitment to sharing the values of respect for the marine environment, safety during sports activities, and the importance of being part of a community that values personal growth through sports and adventure.

For bookings made after cut-off date, full payment will be required immediately upon confirmation of the booking.

### 2. Cancellation Policy:

**a) Cancellation by the participant:**

If you cancel your booking up to 2 months before departure, you will be entitled to request a refund of the amount already paid.

For cancellations made less than 2 months before the start of the experience, the contribution paid will not be refunded, but you can use it as credit to participate in other proposals and experiences organized by the association.  
We still recommend taking out travel insurance that covers this eventuality.

**b) Cancellation by us:**

In the event of extraordinary circumstances, such as natural disasters or a deterioration in the political stability of the destination country, if we deem the safety of the trip compromised, or if the minimum number of participants is not reached within 30 days of the departure date, the participant will have the right to:

Use the contribution paid to participate in other activities or sailing options provided in the association’s seasonal program.  
Request a refund of the amount already paid.

Note: No additional costs or expenses, beyond the contribution paid, will be recognized as compensation for the cancellation of the trip. Therefore, we strongly advise taking out travel insurance to cover any additional costs.

### 3. Travel Requirements and Insurance:

It is your responsibility to arrange personal travel insurance that covers the activities you intend to undertake and provides adequate coverage for delays, cancellations, medical expenses, and personal belongings. We recommend taking out travel insurance that covers possible costs in case of cancellation or postponement of the trip.

It is also your responsibility to have a valid passport, obtain any necessary visas, and meet the health requirements of the destination country.

### 4. Group Conduct:

Guests who behave inappropriately towards the crew, other guests, or local people may be asked to leave the experience.

Inappropriate behavior includes verbal abuse, aggressive attitudes, violence, repeatedly disruptive behavior, excessive consumption of alcohol or drugs, sexual harassment (including unwanted comments or contact), or actions that intimidate, offend, or humiliate a person based on personal characteristics such as age, race, gender, disability, religion, or sexual orientation.

In such cases, no refund will be granted, and all costs related to the early departure will be borne by the guest.
