#### **Introduction to Life on Board**

Learn the basics of living on a catamaran: space organization, water and energy management, for an authentic and responsible maritime experience.

#### **Essential Safety at Sea**

Master key safety procedures, including life jacket use, radio communication, and emergency maneuvers, guided by an experienced skipper.

#### **Navigation Fundamentals**

Learn how to read nautical charts, use GPS, and understand sea orientation principles to become an active part of the crew.

#### **Basic Sailing Techniques**

Participate in sail handling, adjusting the mainsail and jib under the skipper’s supervision, to develop confidence in sailing navigation.

#### **Practical Seamanship Skills**

Gain hands-on experience: tying knots (seaman’s knots), anchoring in a bay, and assisting with mooring operations.

#### **Helm Control**

Take the helm and learn how to maintain course and respond to wind shifts, with personalized guidance.

#### **Understanding Winds and Currents**

Learn how to interpret weather conditions, prevailing winds, and currents in the San Blas area, navigating in harmony with nature.

#### **Route Planning Assistance**

Work alongside the skipper to plan daily routes, balancing weather conditions, distances, and dream destinations.

#### **Maritime Culture**

Immerse yourself in the traditions and language of the sea, enriching your experience with anecdotes and time-honored sailing practices.

#### **Teamwork in Navigation**

Develop a strong team spirit by actively participating in onboard activities, from raising the anchor to coordinating maneuvers, under the guidance of an experienced crew.
