## Description

# **Join the Ikigai Crew**

On board _Ikigai_ , a 14-meter Catana 47 performance catamaran, we don’t just offer unique experiences of sailing, freediving, kitesurfing, and ocean life: we are building a community.

If you feel the call of the ocean and want to challenge yourself, there are two ways to become part of our crew:

## **Volunteers**

Do you want to learn, grow, and gain hands-on experience on board?

We are looking for motivated people who wish to approach the world of sailing and the disciplines we practice: sailing, freediving, kitesurfing, apnea, and onboard life management.
- You will dedicate about 2–3 hours a day to support activities (boat management, small tasks, assisting the crew).
- In return, you will have free boarding and the opportunity to experience the sea as a true protagonist.
- You will share common expenses with all of us by contributing to the onboard fund (_cassa di bordo_).

**Skill Exchange**

We also welcome volunteers who want to contribute with their professional skills instead of daily practical tasks.

If you are a videomaker, storyteller, travel blogger, photographer, or content creator, you can propose a collaboration based on exchange: boarding in return for your services.

This is the perfect opportunity if you have little sea experience and want to learn in the field, or if you want to put your creative talent to use while living and telling a unique adventure.

## **Professionals**

If you already have experience in the nautical sector and want to contribute in a more specific way, we are looking for hostesses and deckhands with skills in boat management and maintenance.
- You can apply as an experienced crew member.
- We offer a dynamic and stimulating environment, where your skills will be valued and put at the service of the project.

## **Why apply?**
- To live the sea as a home, not just as a simple vacation.
- To be part of a project that combines sport, nature, and community.
- To grow as a person and as a sailor, sharing experiences and responsibilities.
- To contribute with your work or creative skills to an authentic and ever-evolving project.

[**Send your application**](https://www.ikigaisailing.com/contact-2/)

Tell us who you are, your experience, and why you want to join Ikigai. Whether you are a volunteer seeking adventure, a creative wishing to exchange your skills, or a professional ready to give concrete support, our crew is waiting for you.

## Logistic

Your journey will most likely begin with your arrival at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) in Panama City, with incoming flights generally scheduled for the afternoon or evening.

For this reason, an overnight stay of at least one night in the capital will be required. This is the perfect opportunity to explore the city and perhaps wander through the charming streets of Casco Viejo, the historic district designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

🕔 The following day, early in the morning, a private 4×4 jeep will pick you up directly at your hotel and take you toward the Caribbean coast.  
From there, after approximately 2.5 hours of travel through the forests of Guna Yala, a local boat will bring you to Ikigai, where you will arrive around 10:30 a.m.

At the end of your stay, on departure day, you will follow the same route in reverse: a local boat will pick you up at dawn directly from Ikigai and take you back to the mainland, where a jeep will transfer you to the airport in time for your return flight.

✈️ The entire journey is organized by us in collaboration with trusted local partners, ensuring a smooth and safe logistics experience from the beginning to the end of your stay.

If you have specific needs (such as early flights, delays, or an extended stay), please let us know so we can find the most suitable solution together.
