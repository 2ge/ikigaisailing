## On-page direct-contact tactics (apply site-wide in Phase 2/3)

1. **"Chat on WhatsApp" button** prominent on mobile + desktop landing pages — high-value bookings start as conversations.
2. **Highlight the non-profit structure** — member contributions, not commercial fees → explains competitive pricing, builds trust.
3. **Address travel hesitations** — detailed guides on logistics, Guna Yala park entry fees, what "all-inclusive" includes → removes friction for direct booking.
